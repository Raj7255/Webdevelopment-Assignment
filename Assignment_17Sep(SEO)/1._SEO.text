Q.> What is seo and explain the importance of seo?

Ans-> SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization, which is the process of optimizing a website to rank higher in search engine results pages (SERPs) through 
various techniques such as keyword research, on-page optimization, link building, and content creation. .
The importance of SEO lies in its ability to increase the visibility and organic traffic of a website, which can ultimately lead to more leads, sales, and profits.
 By optimizing a website for search engines, businesses can reach their target audience more easily and cost-effectively, while also improving their overall online presence.
