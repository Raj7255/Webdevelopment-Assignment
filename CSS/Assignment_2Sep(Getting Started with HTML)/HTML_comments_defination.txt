Q.  Expalin the purpose of comments in HTML?
Ans. In HTML, comments are used to add explanatory notes or remarks within the code. They are ignored by the browser and do not affect the page's display.
   Comments begin with the symbol <!-- and continue untill the end of the line, or are enclosed in pairs of--> to end the comment.
   Comments are useful for documenting code and making it easier to understand for other developers or in the future.
 

There are two way doing comments in HTML.
1. Single-line comment
2. multi-line comments
